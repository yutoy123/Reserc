The problem

Three walls every LMIC researcher hits

Reserc is built to bridge all three.

01

Data is scattered

World Bank, IMF, GSMA, UNDP use different formats and coverage. Finding a number is easy; knowing which source to trust is not.

02

Frameworks are siloed

A student studying food insecurity in Ghana may not know ecological economics or behavioral nudge theory is directly relevant.

03

Data to argument is hard

Knowing mobile money penetration is 60% in Kenya is easy. Knowing what that means for financial inclusion theory is the real work.

Built for developing economy research

Most research tools are built for high-income country data. Reserc is designed from the ground up for the gaps and realities of LMIC data — food systems, climate exposure, informal economies.
